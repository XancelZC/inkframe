Chapter 1 The Newcomer

Tom Walker arrived in the city on a cold Tuesday morning. He carried a single suitcase and a letter of recommendation from his old employer. The streets were crowded with people rushing to work, and Tom felt overwhelmed by the noise and bustle.

"This is it," he said to himself. "A fresh start."

He found the office building on Fifth Street and took a deep breath before entering. The receptionist, a young woman named Jane, looked up from her desk.

"Can I help you?" she asked.

"I'm looking for Mr. Harrison," Tom replied. "I have an appointment."

Chapter 2 The Interview

Mr. Harrison was a tall man with a stern face. He read Tom's letter carefully, then set it down on his desk.

"So you want to work here," he said. It was not a question.

"Yes, sir," Tom answered. "I'm a hard worker, and I learn quickly."

Mr. Harrison studied him for a long moment. "We'll see about that," he finally said. "You start Monday."

Tom couldn't believe his luck. He thanked Mr. Harrison and hurried out of the office. Jane smiled at him as he passed her desk.

"Congratulations," she said. "He doesn't hire just anyone."
